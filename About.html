Our Story

The Heart Behind Zee Caring Companions

At Zee Caring Companions, we believe that great care is about more than meeting daily needs—it's about providing comfort, companionship, dignity, and peace of mind.

Founded by Azeezat Amzat, Zee Caring Companions was created from a genuine passion for helping people live safely, comfortably, and independently in their own homes.

And she also very passionate in looking after Dementia patients due to her personal experience with her father. And she have wide range of experience working in the hospital, community and presently in a Nursing home looking after dementia patients.

Throughout her career, she has cared for people with a wide range of needs, gaining valuable experience while building meaningful relationships with those she supports. These experiences inspired her to establish Zee Caring Companions.

Today, Zee Caring Companions provides trusted home care, companionship, escorted appointments, respite support, and overnight assistance, helping individuals maintain their independence while giving families confidence and reassurance.

Every visit is guided by the values that inspired the company from the beginning:

Enhanced DBS Checked

Fully Insured

Qualified Nurse-Led Care

Proudly Serving Swindon
